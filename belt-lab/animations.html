01FadePowerPoint · Fade

The classic. All cards fade in together, no movement at all. The most universally elegant and the safest — nothing travels across the eye.

02Float InPowerPoint · Float In

Cards rise and fade up one after another (a gentle cascade). PowerPoint's most-loved entrance — refined and premium, never dizzying.

03Fly InPowerPoint · Fly In

Cards fly in from the left in sequence and lock into place. More energetic — one decisive entrance, then perfectly still.

04ZoomPowerPoint · Zoom / Grow

Cards grow from small to full size while fading in. Feels confident and modern — like each project arrives into focus.

05BouncePowerPoint · Bounce

Cards drop in and bounce to a stop. Playful and lively — best if you want a friendlier, less formal feel.

06WipePowerPoint · Wipe

Each card is revealed left-to-right like a brush stroke. Crisp and editorial; pairs beautifully with the gold theme.

07SplitPowerPoint · Split

Cards open from the center outward, like curtains parting. Subtle, classy, a little theatrical.

08SwivelPowerPoint · Swivel

Cards swing in on a 3-D hinge — the same gem move as your motto swivel. Cohesive with the brand, and quietly impressive.

09Blur FocusModern · Focus In

Cards sharpen from a soft blur into clarity. Very contemporary and luxurious — like a camera finding focus.

10Deal the CardsPlayful · Stagger

Cards are dealt one by one with a slight tilt, like a hand of cards laid down. Charismatic and memorable.

11Circle RevealPowerPoint · Shape/Wheel

Each card is revealed through an expanding circle. Distinctive and elegant — a softer cousin of the classic Wheel.

12Grow + PulsePowerPoint · Grow w/ Emphasis

Cards grow in and give one gentle pulse as they settle. Adds a touch of "heartbeat" life without continuous motion.

13Spin InPowerPoint · Spin / Grow&Turn

Cards rotate and scale into place. The most dramatic of the set — use sparingly, but it has real flair.

★Swivel + Blur FocusYour combo · 8 + 9

Exactly what you asked: cards swing in on the 3-D hinge (8) and sharpen from blur (9) at the same time, on one shared clock so both finish together. Merged into a single motion so the two never fight. This is my favorite of the whole lab — cohesive with your motto gem and quietly luxurious.
