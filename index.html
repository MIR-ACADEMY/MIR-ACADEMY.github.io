What we do

We are renovating the future
by educating it.

MIR ACADEMY designs and builds interactive, self-updating learning modules that never go stale. Every course is engineered from scratch — dark-themed, bilingual, built to be used, not just read.

From financial literacy to IT fundamentals to AI & prompting, we build the courses that should already exist. Alongside education, we develop the MIRACDX suite — productivity tools for real daily use on Windows.

The ultimate vision: MIRA Renovator — an AI pipeline that takes outdated documents, textbooks, and dead curricula and outputs modern interactive modules automatically.
